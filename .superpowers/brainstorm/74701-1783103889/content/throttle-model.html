Section 2 — The throttle → scroll model

How stick position becomes scroll motion. This is the core feel.

Stick deflection → scroll speed
1. Deadzone — small center band reads as zero, so a resting thumb won't drift.
2. Proportional — beyond the deadzone, speed rises smoothly with deflection. Push a little = crawl; push all the way = max.
3. Max speed ceiling — full deflection maps to the current max speed. SL lowers it, SR raises it (clamped to a sane range).
4. Direction — up scrolls the script forward (text moves up), down reverses.
5. Motion — every animation frame, position advances by speed × elapsed time, so scrolling is smooth and framerate-independent.
Pause (stick click) freezes motion regardless of stick position — useful to hold your place. Click again to resume the throttle.
